Martha Uhrich Peiffer. Martha Uhrich Peiffer, age 95, passed away peacefully at Willow Valley Retirement Communities in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, on March 5, 2009. Martha was the widow of the Reverend Dr. Harold S. Peiffer. Together they shared a life of Christian ministry that spanned seven decades. Martha was born in Grantville, Pennsylvania, on November 4, 1913. The daughter of D. Valentine and L. Stella Hetrick Uhrich, she was one of five siblings, Helen Uhrich, Ruth Shreffler and Daniel Uhrich, now deceased, and Elenore Barnhart, who resides in Elizabethtown, Pennsylvania. Martha attended school in Grantville, and became a registered nurse in the state of Pennsylvania in 1939. She worked as a nurse at the Masonic Homes and the Elizabethtown Hospital for Crippled Children, and it was there that she met her future husband.
