Flemington Elks plan comedy show April 8. The Flemington Elks Lodge 1928 will host a professional comedy show featuring three comedians on Saturday, April 18, at the Elks lodge, 165 Route 31 South, Flemington. Doors will open at 6 p.m. and the show will start at 8 p.m. following a dinner buffet. Cost is $35 per person, which includes a full dinner buffet with desserts. Due to a high demand, only advance tickets will be sold. For tickets, call Gordy or Linda at (908) 782-6983.
