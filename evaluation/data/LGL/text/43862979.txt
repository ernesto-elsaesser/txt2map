Senior U.S. Military Official Visits Georgia. Gen. Cartwright, who is the second highest ranking military officer of the United States, will meet with President Saakashvili and Defense Minister, Davit Sikharulidze. He will also travel town of Gori, according to the Georgian MoD, and visit the Gori-based first infantry brigade and the first artillery brigade. Gen. Cartwright, vice chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, will travel to Gori-based military unit, according to the Georgian MoD.
